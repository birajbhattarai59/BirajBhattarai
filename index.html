About Biraj Bhattarai

Biraj Bhattarai is an independent musical artist and composer from Nepal. Known for his soulful instrumental tracks, he specializes in blending acoustic clarity with melodic blues and folk influences. His journey began in 2020, where he focused on developing a unique fingerstyle guitar technique and learning the intricacies of digital music distribution.

As a verified artist on platforms like YouTube and Spotify, Biraj has built a global audience purely through independent releases. His music is often described as intimate and expansive, reflecting the landscapes and cultural heartbeat of Nepal through a contemporary lens.

Creative Process

Most compositions are recorded in a home studio environment to preserve the organic, intimate sound of the acoustic guitar. By focusing on percussive fingerstyle and melodic phrasing, each track is designed to evoke deep emotional resonance.
