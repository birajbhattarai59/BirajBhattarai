About Me

Biraj Bhattarai is an instrumental musical artist known for his ability to craft melodies that speak directly to the heart. Rooted in a passion for sound and emotion, Biraj blends acoustic and blues-inspired tones with modern musical expression, creating compositions that are soulful, immersive, and deeply human.

His musical journey began with a fascination for storytelling through sound — using instruments like guitar and piano to express feelings words often can’t. Each composition represents a piece of his personal journey, filled with emotion, reflection, and a love for the art of music itself.

Beyond composing and performing, Biraj is continuously exploring new styles, refining his sound, and connecting with listeners across platforms such as Spotify and YouTube. His dedication to growth and authenticity shines through every note he creates.
