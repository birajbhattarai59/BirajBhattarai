About Biraj Bhattarai

Biraj Bhattarai is an independent musical artist from Nepal. Like many creatives, he started his musical journey in 2020, using the global shift during the pandemic as an opportunity to focus entirely on his passion. ​Without the backing of a major label, Biraj dedicated himself to learning the technical side of the music industry—focusing on digital distribution and consistent releases. This dedication has paid off, as he has successfully built a verified presence on global platforms like YouTube and Google, reaching listeners internationally. ​Biraj produces music that blends personal expression with modern digital sounds. He views his growing streaming numbers not just as statistics, but as proof that independent artists can build a sustainable path purely through connection with their audience. ​He continues to release new music and is focused on expanding his creative horizons.

Creative Process

I record most of my compositions in a home studio environment, focusing on the organic sound of the guitar. By blending techniques like percussive fingerstyle with melodic phrasing, I aim to create music that feels both intimate and expansive.
