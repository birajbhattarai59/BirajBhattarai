About BIRAJ BHATTARAI

Biraj Bhattarai is an instrumental musical artist known for his talent in composing and performing captivating melodies. He has gained recognition for his unique style and soulful compositions.

He discovered his passion for music at a young age. His upbringing played a crucial role in fostering his love for music and encouraged him to pursue a career in the industry.
